Instructions:

1. Add the accounts you want to scrape on usernames.txt
2. Run Followings.py, it will ask your cookies.
3. Log ing to your instagram account (recommend to use a spare account), extract your cookies as Header String
4. After finishing to scrape the accounts, some files will be generated on scraped folder
5. Run processdata.py to format the information to be imported to Gephi
6. Your nodes and edges will be found on gephi_input
7. Input your graph data to Gephi.
8. HAVE FUN!
